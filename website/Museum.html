Home to the greatest collection of operational vintage computers in the world, the National Museum of Computing is a museum located in the United Kingdom that specializes in the preservation and restoration of historic computer systems. The Milton Keynes, Buckinghamshire, museum is situated on Bletchley Park. Having held six of the ten Colossus computers in service at the end of World War II, it was the first purpose-built computer center in the world when it opened in 2007  .
